# FINLY — Financial Management SaaS

> Multi-tenant web platform for financial management of micro and small businesses.

## Overview

FINLY is a SaaS application designed to centralize the financial operation of small businesses in a single platform — eliminating the need for multiple spreadsheets and disconnected tools.

The system covers the complete financial cycle: contract registration, accounts payable and receivable, cash flow projection, income statement (DRE), and document management — all in a multi-tenant architecture with team collaboration features.

## Key Features

- **Contracts** — register, version, and track service contracts with clients
- **Accounts Payable/Receivable** — categorized entries with due dates, status, and payment history
- **Cash Flow Projection** — rolling timeline view of expected inflows and outflows
- **DRE (Income Statement)** — automated generation from registered transactions
- **Document Management** — attach and organize PDFs per contract or transaction
- **Multi-tenant** — isolated workspaces per company, role-based access
