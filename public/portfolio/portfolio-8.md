# Grupo BIM — Process Quality & Data Reliability Systems

## Overview

Grupo BIM operates in engineering and infrastructure services, requiring structured internal processes and reliable operational data.

This project involved the development and evolution of internal systems designed to improve **process traceability, operational consistency, and data reliability**.

## My Role

I worked on designing and improving internal tools used to control operational workflows and business data.

Main responsibilities included:

- Development of structured dashboards and validated spreadsheet models
- Implementation of data validation rules and auditing mechanisms
- Automation of repetitive operational tasks
- Development of scripts to consolidate and analyze operational data

## Quality Approach

The main goal was to apply **Quality Assurance principles to operational processes**, reducing human errors and improving reliability of internal data.

Quality practices included:

- Data validation rules
- Process standardization
- Automated report generation
- Traceability of operational inputs and outputs

## Technologies

- Python
- JavaScript
- Spreadsheet automation
- Data validation pipelines
- Process dashboards

## Key Contributions

- Reduced operational errors through automated validation systems
- Improved data reliability across internal workflows
- Implemented automated reporting processes
- Increased traceability of engineering and administrative operations
